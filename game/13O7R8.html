Orbia – Award-Winning Casual Game for Endless Fun and Challenge!

◆ Google Play Awards: Most Casual ◆

Orbia invites you and your friends into a vibrant, unique world filled with hundreds of thrilling levels. Your mission? Navigate through dynamic obstacles with precision – a perfectly timed tap is all it takes to advance!

GAMEPLAY
Designed for players of all skill levels, Orbia’s easy-to-pick-up mechanics make it fun from the first tap. Master your timing as levels increase in complexity, collect powerful bonuses, and chain together combos for maximum rewards.

ART
Immerse yourself in stunning minimalistic graphics and vivid colors, creating a visually captivating experience that’s as relaxing as it is exciting.

SOUND
Enjoy a fully immersive experience with carefully crafted sound effects and beautiful soundtracks that accompany you every step of the way.

CONTENT MADNESS
Explore hundreds of unique levels across multiple worlds, each with fresh challenges and new experiences.

CHARACTERS
Discover and unlock a wide range of heroes, each representing a different world and featuring distinct abilities. Customize with tons of unique skins to make every adventure your own!
